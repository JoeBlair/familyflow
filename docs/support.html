Common questions

  • How do we share a family? One person creates the family, then shares the invite code (Family tab → Share). Others sign up and join with that code.
  • How do I add a child or a cleaner? Family tab → Add member, then pick the type: Family, Child, or Home help.
  • How do I delete my account? Family tab → Delete account. This removes your account and data; if you’re the last member, the family is deleted too.
  • Does it sync across phones? Yes — everyone in the family sees the same lists, updated in real time.
